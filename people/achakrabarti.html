Background

Arnab Chakrabarti received his engineering (B.Sc) degree in Computer Science in 2010 from West Bengal University of Technology, Kolkata (India) and his master's degree in Computer Science (Software Systems Engineering) in June 2013 from RWTH Aachen University. He worked as a research assistant in the Life Science Informatics group at the Fraunhofer Institute for Applied Information Technology (FIT) in St. Augustin, Germany from July 2015 to June 2019. Afterwards, he worked as a research assistant and a PhD student at the Chair for Computer Science 5 at RWTH Aachen University. During this time, he also held the position of the Study Coordinator for the international Master's programme Media Informatics jointly offered by RWTH Aachen University and BiT Bonn.

Currently, he is working as a Data Scientist and a Post Doctoral Researcher at the Center for Computational Biology, RWTH Aachen University. Alongside he is also affiliated as a Research Assistant at the Center for Integrated Onkologie in Uniklikin Köln.

Research Interests
  • High dimensional data analysis
  • Visualization and visual analytics
  • Data lake and data spaces
  • Data management, data engineering, data integration and metadata management
